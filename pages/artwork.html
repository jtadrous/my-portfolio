Chair Sculpture

Wood Shop Project, 2020

An old, thrifted chair reinvented into a cozy, lounge chair for any home. This Chair Sculpture stands to express the emotion of bright, child-like happiness. It was created by fully disassembling the parts of an old chair, cutting and fastening them together in a shape similar to a rocking chair, and painting the wood a neutral white to let the teal upholstery pop. Broken CDs serve as the detailing on the arm rests to add some shine.

Everlasting Emblems

Logo and Stationary Project, 2018

A fictional company logo and full stationary set with a teal accent color to match. Everlasting Emblems symbolizes any sort of Logo-Maker or Graphic Design company. The full stationary set was all created using Adobe Illustrator, and includes an envelope, business card, and letterhead.

Drawings + Paintings

Beginning Drawing and Painting Coursework, 2019

A series of artwork created during the Beginning Drawing I and Beginning Painting I courses offered at the Herron School of Art. Most paintings were created using oil-based paints and fine brushes. All drawings were created using charcoal sticks and pencils.

Mafiacent

Genre Shift Project, 2021

A creative take on the Disney movie known as Maleficent. For this project, we were tasked with researching two different movie genres. We had to chose a movie from one of the genres and design a movie poster that would fit the second genre. Here I took the fantasy movie Maleficent and transformed it into the gangster genre. This was all designed in Adobe Photoshop.

Investigation Exploration

Movie Poster Project, 2018

A series of four different movie posters, made using Adobe Illustrator, depicting the same fictional movie, Investigation Exploration. Investigation Exploration is supposed to be an action movie of three sisters moving to the streets of Egypt for the summer to find happiness and passion. Each movie poster was made with a different aspect of the movie in mind and each has its own voice.

Bubble Bobble

Stop Motion Animation, 2018

A stop motion animation of the iconic Bubble Bobble game. All of the graphics were printed out and placed on a board. The video features 362 different images, which were edited and sequenced in Adobe After Effects.

Maralumi Chocolate

Typography Logotypes Project, 2021

A unique logotype of a chosen word crafted using typography principles. Through research, semiotic nuances of the word were identified and given a visual voice. Topics considered during the logotype development include type characters, letterform, style, texture, weight, emotional tone, etc.

Fictional Company Logos

Logo Making Project, 2018

A collection of four different company logos. North Beacon, Forward Thinking, and Side Slice are all fictitious companies created for the purpose of portraying a company persona through a logo. Each of these logos were made with Adobe Illustrator using various negative space and coloring techniques.
