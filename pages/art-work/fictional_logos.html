Fictional Company Logos

Logo Making Project, 2018

A collection of four different company logos. North Beacon, Forward Thinking, and Side Slice are all fictitious companies created for the purpose of portraying a company persona through a logo. Each of these logos were made with Adobe Illustrator using various negative space and coloring techniques.
