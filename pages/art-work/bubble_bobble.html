Bubble Bobble

Stop Motion Animation, 2018

A stop motion animation of the iconic Bubble Bobble game. This project was intended for us to recreate our favorite vintage video games through the use of stop motion. All of the graphics were printed out and placed on a board. The video features 362 different images, which were edited and sequenced in Adobe After Effects.
