Keurig Website Re-creation

N315 Advanced Multi-Device Web Development Final Project, 2021


A re-creation of the Keurig e-commerce site that sells coffee makers and allows users to sign up with their first name, last name, email, and password. They can also login and logout easily based on user feedback. Once a user is logged in, they are able to add multiple items to their cart, remove items from their cart, and checkout. This website is fully responsive and uses various development tools such as Firebase.
