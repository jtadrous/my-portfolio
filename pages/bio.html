Julie Tadrous

Aspiring Web Designer based in Indianapolis

Julie is a dependable and hard-working designer with training in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. With a record of academic and extracurricular success, she has completed many web-based projects and design works. She has also built strong communication, time management, and teamwork skills working with various students and customers. In her free time, she loves to paint and work with colors. She hopes to pursue a career in Web Design and Development in her future.

Education

Julie attended Indiana University - Purdue University Indianapolis starting in August 2018. She has completed 97 credits toward a double major in Informatics (B.S.) and Media Arts and Science (B.S.) and maintained a cumulative 4.0 GPA. She also has a specialization in Web Design & Development and a Studio Arts & Technology Minor.

Experience

Julie has applicable experience within the IT field, from final course projects focused on developing web-based solutions to everyday problems to working as an ADA Website Compliance Intern for REN-ISAC. In her internship, she collaborated with her team to design appealing and user-friendly web pages for the REN-ISAC site, and analyzed online and written content for ADA Compliance. She has also worked as a tutor for the School of Informatics and Computing, teaching mathematics, including Finite and Logic.

Skills

From these experiences, Julie has gained a combination of leadership, organization, and teamwork skills. She has also taken multiple college courses to learn programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, and MySQL. For her artwork, she has worked with various programs including Autodesk Maya, Blender, and Adobe Photoshop.
